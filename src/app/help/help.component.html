Mixed Letters

Goal: Rearrange the scrambled letters to form a correct English word.
How to Play: Click and drag the letters to change their order until you form a correct word.

Trivia

Goal: Answer trivia questions in English to earn points.
How to Play: Read the displayed question and choose the correct answer from several options.

Word Sorter

Goal: Sort the words in the correct order, such as alphabetically or by specific categories.
How to Play: Drag the words or click on them to arrange them according to the given criteria.
